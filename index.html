Offensive Impact:
[(Points + (Assists * 2)) / Minutes Played] * (Field-Goal % * 0.5 + 3-Point % * 0.25 + Free-Throw % * 0.25) - (Turnovers / Minutes Played)
Defensive Impact:
(Steals + Blocks + 0.5 * Rebounds) / Minutes Played (only players with at least 10 minutes played per game)
Playmaking Ability:
Assists / Turnovers (only players with at least 1 turnover per game)
Scoring Threat:
Points / (2 * (Field-Goal Attemps + 0.44 * Free-Throw Attemps)) * Points
Reliability:
(Minnutes Played / 40) * [(Field-Goal % * Points) + (Steals + Blocks) * 2
